\[\Huge{e}\Large{\mathrm{xpressionizr}}\]

We’re stoked you want to try \(\large{e}\)xpressionizr!

An innovative mobile/web app that returns data on your expressions and equations.

\(\large{e}\)xpressionizr can information from taking pictures, handwriting, or typing equations. The app will use OCR to seperate your characters from one another using Euclidean distance and run each one individually to determine what it is. After its analysis, the app will output \(\LaTeX\) code and pull from the Wolfram|Alpha API to give you data.

Potential applications of this app include solving homework questions, getting information on theroems and constants, and graphing.

The app can be expanded to included user-specific handwriting recognition and learning one’s specific handwriting.
